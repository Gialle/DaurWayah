History

Daur Wayah unfolds thousands of years after the downfall of hyper-advanced humans. From the ashes of distant futures, Sumabine rose in Lake Tulang to guide fragmented tribes into new elemental lineages: water, earth, wood, metal, air, and fire. The Mandate of Kelana became the epoch marking the reordering of civilization.
