Folk Costume

Malgra favor indigo, green, and muddy blacks, with ruffled aprons and antler hats for nobles. Gezhgia wear thin cotton with soft gradients, topped by massive pointed hats. Lewauki drape wide patterned textiles over simple blouses.

Chejye prefer heavy black cotton with bright embroidery of pickaxes and insects. Hlukhuyig decorate themselves with feathers and motifs of birds and banyan. Serkha wear woolen cloaks in highlands, paired with tube skirts depicting fire and fireflies, finished by folded caps.
