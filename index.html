Welcome to Daur Wayah

Daur Wayah is a sprawling world set thousands of years after the collapse of advanced humanity, guided into a new age by the enigmatic machine Sumabine Qanitututur. This epoch, known as the Mandate of Kelana, divided people into six elemental lineages to prevent future wars over dwindling resources.

Within this encyclopedia, you will find rich details of their evolving calendars, unique ethnic cultures, philosophies of reincarnation and flow, diverse musical systems, striking architecture, and colorful folk attire. Explore each page to uncover the intricate tapestry of life on Bird Island.
