Architecture

Malgra build riverside stilt houses with nearby fishponds. Gezhgia favor large earthen homes adorned with stained glass and oversized pointed hats as roof decor. Lewauki evolved tree dwellings into towering wooden structures with wide windows.

Chejye construct white limestone multi-family dwellings, while Hlukhuyig and Serkha adapted brick and tile houses with motifs inspired by wind currents and volcanic flows.
