Ethnicities & Regions

There are six primary ethnicities in Daur Wayah, each aligned with an elemental aspect:

  • Malgra (Water): river dwellers, raised houses, fish and flood rituals.
  • Gezhgia (Earth): savannah settlers, ceramic artisans, livestock keepers.
  • Lewauki (Wood): forest canopy people, evolved tree houses into towering wooden homes.
  • Chejye (Metal): mountain miners, white limestone homes, multi-family complexes.
  • Hlukhuyig (Air): plateau archers, wind turbines, kite ceremonies.
  • Serkha (Fire): volcanic slopes, sulfur and eternal flame rituals.
