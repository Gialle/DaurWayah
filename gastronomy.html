Döröe Philosophy & Cosmology

In Daur Wayah, the guiding principle of life is Döröe, which teaches that existence is a fluid cycle, never strictly linear. Souls are not bound by forward time; they may reincarnate into the past, leap to far futures, or awaken in parallel epochs. This constant movement prevents what the people fear most: cosmic stagnation.

Key ideas include the Memory River, a metaphysical current carrying echoes of previous lives that surface as déjà vu or deep empathy across ethnic lines. Rituals of Spirit Backflow intentionally merge young and old souls to maintain balance. Occasionally, entire eras are defined by Cosmic Waves—periods dominated by youthful, innovative spirits or by the gravitas of ancient souls steering society toward caution and tradition.
